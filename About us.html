Welcome to Meal Connect

Feeding India is a non-profit organization dedicated to eradicating hunger and improving malnutrition outcomes in India.

We work toward this mission by supporting large-scale systemic interventions as well as providing essential food support to underserved communities in the form of raw grains and freshly cooked food.

Feeding India works with on-ground non-profit partners working on education and child/maternal malnutrition by providing regular meals to dependents.

At Feeding India, we understand the critical issue of hunger and its impact on individuals and communities. Through our initiatives, we strive to create a sustainable and equitable food system that ensures no one goes hungry. We believe in the power of collective action and collaboration to bring about positive change.

Join us in our mission to eradicate hunger and improve malnutrition outcomes in India. Together, we can make a significant difference in the lives of those in need and build a brighter future for all.
